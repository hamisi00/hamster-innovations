What We Do

Pragmatic, local-first software that just works — even when the internet doesn't

Many Kenyan businesses, organizations, and communities still manage operations using spreadsheets and paper. That causes lost sales, slow reconciliation, and fragile operations when connectivity fails.

We turn messy workflows into reliable, desktop-first applications. From retail shops to gyms, chamas, and property management — we build systems that handle records, transactions, and analytics with confidence.
